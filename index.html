About Me

I build the platforms people rely on.

I'm a product and UX leader who has spent over a decade helping entertainment companies — studios, networks, and streaming platforms — work smarter through technology. Most recently as VP of UX and Accounting Product Management at Cast & Crew, I led cross-functional teams delivering payroll and production operations software used by the industry's biggest names.

My work lives at the intersection of enterprise workflow, human-centered design, and operational efficiency. I don't just ship features — I align teams, modernize legacy systems, and build the organizational muscle for sustainable product growth.

I'm currently exploring my next senior leadership opportunity. If you're building something where design rigor and product strategy matter, I'd love to connect.
